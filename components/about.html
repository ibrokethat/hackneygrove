About

Located in Brixton, South London, our practice was set up in 2012 by Sarah Hackney.

We undertake high quality residential architecture and interior design, with a mindful attitude to sustainable design.

Our range of work can involve anything from a whole house approach, to extensions, loft conversions, kitchens, bathrooms or even eco-retrofitting.

Within this framework we offer feasibility studies, planning and listed building applications, building control submissions, interior fit-outs as required, or can offer them as stand alone services if preferred.
